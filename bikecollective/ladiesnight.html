Monday is for the ladies

Ladies Night will be every Monday from 6:30pm–9pm starting April 9th. If you are interested in riding with a partner, you can meet volunteer Carolyn in the plaza of the Federal Building on 310 W. Wisconsin Ave. at 6pm and ride out to the Bike Collective together.

Who is invited to Ladies Night?

One out of every ten cyclists in Milwaukee is a woman. Ladies Night is meant to be an environment in which women and female-identified folks can feel safe volunteering at the shop.
