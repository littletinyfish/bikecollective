Monday is for the ladies

Ladies Night will be every Monday from 6:30pm – 9pm. If you are interested in riding with a partner, email us and we’ll help you find a buddy to ride out to the Bike Collective together.

Who is invited to Ladies Night?

The mechanics world is dominated by men. The sports world is dominated by men. As a result, one out of every ten cyclists in Milwaukee is a woman. Ladies Night is meant to be an environment in which women and female‑identified folks can feel safe learning and volunteering at the shop.

Can women volunteer on other days besides Monday?

Of course! We work very hard to be accomodating to the needs of all types of people; women are welcome in the shop at any time. Ladies Night should be considered an additional resource.

Need more women‑oriented bicycle resources?

Coast In Bikes also has a DIY Ladies Night every Wednesday.
